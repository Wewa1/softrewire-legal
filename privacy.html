Privacy Policy — Andel App

At Andel, your privacy is important to us. We collect only minimal information necessary to operate the app and improve user experience.

What we collect

  • Basic TikTok profile data (username, profile image, user ID) after login.
  • Optional metadata about videos (title, description, hashtags) you create inside Andel.

What we do not collect

  • No personal messages or passwords.
  • No background tracking or automated actions.

How we use the data

  • To display your content correctly inside Andel.
  • To let you share content you choose on TikTok or other linked platforms.

You can revoke access at any time via your TikTok account settings.

For any privacy-related inquiries, contact privacy@softrewire.com.
